Improving global implementation of:

Genomic Medicine in Clinical Care

The Global Genomic Medicine Collaborative (G2MC) was established to recognize and harness activities implementing and shaping genomic medicine around the globe.

The main goals of the Global Genomic Medicine Collaborative are to:
  • Create a vibrant community of global leaders in genomic medicine implemntation
  • Capture, share, and disseminate best practices for genomic medicine
  • Serve as nexus, clearing house, and knowledge base for genomic medicine activities
  • Develop opportunities for collaborative genomic medicine demonstration projects focused on clinical implementation and outcomes research
  • Asist our members in developing strategies for clinical implementation of genomic medicine and evidence generation to suport its sustainability

How We Work

G2MC formed six working groups to support its activities:

  • Education
  • Evidence
  • IT/Bioinformatics
  • National Programs and Implementation
  • Pharmacogenomics (PGx)
  • Policy
